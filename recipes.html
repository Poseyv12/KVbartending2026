Signature cocktails you can recreate at home

Sunset Margarita

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Tequila
  • 1 oz Triple Sec
  • 1 oz Lime Juice
  • 1/2 oz Orange Juice
  • Salt for rimming
  • Lime slice for garnish

Instructions

Rim the glass with salt. Shake tequila, triple sec, lime juice, and orange juice with ice. Strain into the glass over ice and garnish with a lime slice.

Berry Basil Smash

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Gin
  • 1/2 oz Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 oz Simple Syrup
  • Fresh Berries
  • Fresh Basil
  • Soda Water

Instructions

Muddle fresh berries and basil in a shaker. Add gin, lemon juice, and simple syrup, then shake with ice. Strain into a glass over ice and top with soda water.

Spicy Paloma

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Tequila
  • 1/2 oz Lime Juice
  • Grapefruit Soda
  • Jalapeño slices
  • Chili Salt for rimming
  • Lime wedge for garnish

Instructions

Rim the glass with chili salt. Muddle jalapeño slices in a shaker. Add tequila and lime juice, then shake with ice. Strain into the glass over ice and top with grapefruit soda. Garnish with a lime wedge.

Classic Mojito

Ingredients

  • 2 oz White Rum
  • 1 oz Lime Juice
  • 2 tsp Sugar
  • Fresh Mint Leaves
  • Sparkling Water
  • Mint sprig for garnish

Instructions

Muddle mint leaves with sugar and lime juice in a glass. Add rum and fill the glass with ice. Top with sparkling water and garnish with a mint sprig.
